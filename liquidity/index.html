    Adding liquidity to decentralized pools

    Magnolia is a non-premined project, i.e. both the developers and the entire Magnolia team do not own NOLIA tokens, as these are in the hands of community of miners.

    This is why the project depends exclusively on its users, both for mining and for adding liquidity to the pools of decentralized exchanges so that the token can be exchanged.

    In exchange for adding liquidity to the decentralized pool, the user will receive SLP tokens, which he can use to withdraw his portion of the pool whenever he wants, or to stake them and receive MAG (Magnolia Governance) tokens, which will be used in the near future to vote on community decisions and thus choose the direction of the project. the project.

    To add liquidity to decentralized pools, simply follow the instructions below:

    Send your NOLIA to the Harmony Network (ONE)

    You can send your NOLIA to the Harmony network through the Bridge website enabled for this purpose: link

    Get ONE tokens

    To add liquidity to the pool, you need to get ONE tokens on the Harmony network. You can get them on exchanges like Binance or Huobi.

    Add liquidity in Sushi Swap

    Once you have both tokens on the Harmony network, you can add liquidity in the following link: ONE-NOLIA Pool

    If you need help in the process, you can consult us through our Discord or follow the instructions from the Sushi Swap documentation.

    After that, you will get the corresponding amount of SLP tokens.
